Gessner Mom

1: First I need to pick up my daughter from her cheer class at Birons Youth Sports Center. I get out of the parking lot and turn right onto the frontage road. I change lanes to the left four times to take the on-ramp onto I-10. But I’ll need to take the Bunker Hill Rd exit soon, so I stay in the rightmost lane. (*) 2: I need to grab donuts for a party later today, so I need to go to Shipley. To do this, I take the off-ramp to Bunker Hill Rd, passing underneath an on-ramp. Once I'm on the frontage road, I change lanes to the right to turn right after I see a Ciro’s. I then drive through the parking lot to pick up my sweets. I then leave the parking lot, turning right on Bunker Hill Rd, and turn right onto Barryknoll Ln.
3: I then turn right on Frostwood Dr, left on Kingsride Ln, and right on Benignus Rd, and turn right once again onto the frontage road. I change lanes to the left four times again to take the on-ramp onto I-10. But this time, as soon as I merge onto the highway, I change lanes another four times to drive on the second-to-left lane. (*) 4: I need to grab some tortillas from H-E-B so I realize I actually need to take the Bunker Hill Rd exit again, so I switch four lanes to the right, take the exit ramp onto the frontage road, turn left on Bunker Hill Rd, and turn left again after passing underneath the highway into the H-E-B parking lot. Once I pick up my stuff, I leave the parking lot turning right back onto Bunker Hill Rd, and turn right onto the frontage road. I immediately switch lanes twice to the left, to the leftmost lane that will not take me onto the on-ramp. (*)
5: I need to grab lunch for my son at Torchy’s, so I switch two lanes to the right and turn right onto a driveway behind a parking garage towards Mathewson Ln. I cross Mathewson Ln into the Torchy’s parking lot, with Memorial District Park on my left. Once I grab my food, I turn left onto Mathewson Ln out of the parking lot, and go straight at the Gessner Rd light, into another parking lot. 6: I realize I don’t want to get a car wash, so I make a U-turn and turn left onto Gessner Road. I turn right onto the frontage road, and immediately switch lanes to the left two times. (*)
7: I need to pick up some kimchi from Doosan, so I turn right onto Conrad Sauer Dr and turn right into the parking lot. After grabbing what I need, I turn right out of the parking lot onto Westview Dr. 8: I need to grab a new lightbulb from Home Depot. I turn right onto Bunker Hill Rd and turn right onto the frontage road. I then immediately change lanes all the way to the left to take the on-ramp onto I-10. Once I merge, I switch lanes once to the left because I’m going to exit soon, but not onto the Sam Houston Tollway. (*)
9: I keep left at the split and then take the off-ramp and, after merging with the frontage road, turn right into the parking lot for Home Depot. I grab what I need and turn left out of the parking lot onto Lumpkin Road. I need to make one last stop today to return some clothes at Marshalls. I turn right onto the frontage road and immediately change lanes all the way to the left, and take the Texas U-turn to the eastbound frontage road. I take the on-ramp onto I-10 and change one lane to the left. (*) 10: After changing lanes to the right again, I take the exit to the frontage road and turn right into the Marshalls parking lot, where I will complete my final errand for the day.
