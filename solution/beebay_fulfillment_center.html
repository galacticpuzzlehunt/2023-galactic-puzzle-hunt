Solution: beeBay Fulfillment Center

Written by Violet Xiao and Anderson Wang

This is a sequence of 13 levels that mix Sokoban mechanics with the mechanics of the card game.

Here is a solving video of the 13 levels.

A quick text summary of each level:

Level 1: Push the Friendly Battle Bot to the middle column.

Level 2: Push an Enemy Battle Bot to the middle column, letting it destroy our 0-Power bot. This frees up room to push a Friendly Battle Bot into the middle column.

Level 3: Go up and around the boxes to be able to move them out of the way and get our Friendly Battle Bot to the middle column.

Level 4: Put our Friendly Battle Bot in the middle column, in front of (protecting) the Worker Bot. On the next turn, push the R3C2 and R3C4 enemy bots to the sides, push the R2C2 and R2C4 enemy bots to the back row, and then push the R2C3 enemy bot so it is no longer in front of the enemy Camp.

Level 5: Use the Chicken and Friendly Battle Bot to attack, and the Worker Bot to block a hit.

Level 6: Zap the bottom enemy bot of the middle column, then use the Worker Bot to block hits in the fourth column, then go back to the middle column.

Level 7: Zap the bottom enemy bot of the 5th column, then block for our bots in the fourth column. Zap our bottom allied (!) bot in the 4th column and push the friendly bot to the 5th column.

Level 8: On turn 1, destroy the enemy 8/1 Enemy Battle Bot, and move + push boars to block the 3 remaining enemy bots. On turn 2, destroy the 3/3 Enemy Bot and weaken the 4/5 Enemy Bot. On turn 3, destroy the 4-Power Enemy Bot. On turn 4, move + push our 3-Power boar to the right column, and use the Worker Bot to block for it. Now, use our boar to slowly destroy every remaining enemy.

Level 9: Use the Box on the rightmost column. On turn 1, have the explosives originally in column 1 take a hit while setting up the explosives in column 2. On turn 2, push those column 2 explosives to the space below the enemy bot, while setting up the explosives in column 1. On turn 3, swap + move the explosives originally in column 1 so that they complete the explosive chain. Now we are under no time pressure to similarly free the two other explosives to destroy the right side.

Level 10: The explosives with 5 or 6 health can be weakened with our Friendly Battle Bot over several turns. Set up a chain with 2 explosives in column 1, 1 in column 2, 1 in column 3, and 2 in column 4.

Level 11: Use bots 3 and 4 on the left, and bots 1, 2, 5, 6, and the Worker Bot on the right.

Level 12: Push + move + push the Chicken into enemy territory, then use the Worker Bot to block a hit. Use the Chicken to directly attack the Camp.

Level 13: On the first turn, have the Friendly Battle Bot trade hits with the enemy bot, Zap the enemy camp, use Eager Beever to protect our camp. On the second turn, push the weakened enemy bot next to the enemy camp, push Eager Beever where our Friendly Battle Bot used to be, use that Beever’s positioning to be able to push Explosives up directly below the weakened enemy bot, then move the Beever so that we can push it to protect our camp.

Author’s Notes

I knew pretty early on that I wanted a mashup of our card game and sokoban to work. Some of the early ideas we had involved other mechanics like multi-pushing, actually playing cards or bringing a deck, a bigger grid size, and having the goal be more sokoban-like, with explicit goals to push things into.

I do wish that the user experience could be more streamlined--I'm sure people used to playing grid-based puzzle games found all the extra button presses/mouse clicks to be annoying. That being said, I'm still very glad with how the puzzle turned out.

Many thanks to Brian for the card art for this battle! And my eternal gratitude for Anderson for all his work on this puzzle--he made the Boarry Farmer and Eager Beever levels, and perhaps more importantly gave me the assurance to believe that we could actually make this puzzle.

--Violet
