Solution: Two Worlds Collide
Answer: PLASTIC

Written by Brian Chen

Each clue clues a word or phrase that is both a card in Dominion and a card in Magic: the Gathering. As suggested by the flavortext, take the absolute difference between their costs in those two games (taking the Converted Mana Cost for MtG cards to produce a single integer). Then index into the word or phrase to obtain the answer, PLASTIC.
