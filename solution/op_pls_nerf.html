Solution: OP pls nerf
Answer: TICKET

Written by Dai Yang

We are presented with a tier list of various images. There are 12 items in the tier list, and 12 animal emojis below the tier list, which suggests that we should identify animals corresponding to the keywords depicted by the images.

Tier Animal
S CANE TOAD, MONITOR LIZARD
A COTTONMOUTH SNAKE, MAKO SHARK
B SPITTING SPIDER, THRESHER SHARK
C ARCHERFISH, CATFISH, OGRE SPIDER
D BASILISK LIZARD
F KIWI BIRD, TREE FROG

As the flavor text suggests, these animals all appear in videos by TierZoo, a YouTube channel dedicated to teaching zoology by ranking animals based on their evolutionary adaptability, as seen through the lens of competitive video games. As confirmation, each animal appears in the same tier in their respective video, e.g. the ogre spider appears in The Spider Tier List ranked at C tier.

At the bottom of the puzzle, there are patch notes with 12 balance changes. These changes either increase or decrease a parameter by a multiple of 10%. The key is that each balance change is a targeted buff or nerf toward a specific animal. For example, the ogre spider lacks irises so its retinas burn during daylight hours, a trait that is also mentioned in its TierZoo video, which implies that “Daylight hours increased by 10%” is a nerf to the ogre spider, thereby lowering its tier placement. We associate each balance change with an animal, then move that animal up or down 1 tier for 10%, 2 tiers for 20%, and so on.

We notice that after the patch, there are exactly 2 animals in each tier. Furthermore, those 2 animals share exactly 1 common letter in their keyword, e.g. CANE and OGRE share the letter E.

Reading the common letters in descending tier order gives us the answer TICKET.
