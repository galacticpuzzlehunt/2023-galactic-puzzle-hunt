Solution: Asteroid

Written by Violet Xiao

This battle features three sub-battles where the enemy is an asteroid (or asteroids) that must be addressed in a set amount of turns, but do not directly attack the player. In general, it is helpful in these battles to spend the first few turns deploying low cost Creatures in order to have access to more Food sooner.

Battle 1: Apocalyptic Asteroid

This battle requires us to do 85 damage in 10 turns. This can be done with low-cost Creatures to ramp up Food, plus units that have high Power for their cost, such as Boarry Farmer, Queen of Bison, Hog, or Lady Jane. The Queen of Bison is particularly powerful here. It is also possible to strategically place units for re to buff, or for Nice Cow to repeatedly buff.

Battle 2: Asteroid Fragments

This battle requires us to destroy five units with 25 Health each in 10 turns. Treating this battle similarly to getting 125 single-target damage can work, with similar strategies as Battle 1, especially if Queen of Bison or Nice Cow are used. It is also possible to take advantage of Creatures that can hit multiple units, such as Quail Aboard Fiery Steeds, or Oklahoma Tusks. These Creatures benefit from using Beeowulf to position the Asteroid Fragments closer, and from additional buffs from cards such as Tusk Enhancement or Nice Cow.

Battle 3: Meaty Ore

Unlike the previous two battles, this battle requires us just to summon Creatures with enough health (123) to absorb the blow of the Meaty Ore. Again, deploying low-cost Creatures at first will be most effective, and in this battle, it can be helpful to eventually move some of our units up to give more room to summon Creatures.

Creatures that are good for this battle include anything with high health, Dino Nugget (if comboed with other Green units), o (if comboed with cows), and Eager Beever (to move Creatures more easily). One particularly good unit is Rude Cow, whose special can be used turn after turn to gain 4 extra health. Rude Cow is especially potent with two Boarnana Trees—if a Rude Cow is used to buff the health of at least one Boarnana Tree, and the two Boarnana Trees are in the same row, their Flex can be repeatedly used to gain a net of 3 health per Boarry available every turn.

Author’s Notes

This battle was one of the first pure card battles to be made. It originally only had the first part, and was themed as a mad scientist's doomsday device.

I was hesitant to actually include this in the hunt, but later on in the process, we had a need for more pure card battles, so I tried out adding the other variants (stage 2 and stage 3). We also wanted to address an imbalance in the number of battles the dino region had, so this became the natural enemy of the dinosaurs.

The asteroid, of course, is canonically the same asteroid that appears in GPH 2022.

And also, many thanks to Brian for drawing the card art for this battle!
