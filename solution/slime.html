Solution: Slime

Written by Lewis Chen

This puzzle begins with a single Slime. When each slime dies, it produces up to two smaller slimes, one of which has the Speedy ability (“This slime likes to dash!”: it attacks during the first AI turn after its spawn, and moves every turn) and one of which has the Poisonous ability (“This slime deals Damage Over Time!”: it inflicts 1 Poison when attacking; at the start of each turn, our Poisoned units take damage equal to their Poison counters, which then decrease by 1). Each slime has a different color.

For gathering data, the Creature King Bee (who sends his regards) is very powerful. Attacking Slime with King Bee three times makes Slime’s Power 0, and thus makes it unable to attack. Once Slime can’t attack, we have as many turns as we want to set up for the remainder of the fight. In the remaining fight, King Bee allows us to kill all slimes without them having a chance to attack: whenever we split a slime we can attack it with two copies of King Bee to set its Power to 0. The Poisonous slime can’t attack the turn after it’s created, so we can then set its Power to 0 the next turn without taking a hit. Moving King Bee into enemy territory so it becomes Sneaky makes sure that slimes can’t ever become inaccessible by being stuck behind other slimes.

There are 26 total slimes with a color name, which suggests matching the set of slimes with an encoding of the alphabet. The text for the Poisonous ability suspiciously capitalizes “Damage Over Time”, suggesting the abbreviation DOT, and the text for the Speedy ability uses the word “dash”, suggesting that this may match up with Morse code. When trying to solve the puzzle, the strategy we used for data collection doesn’t quite work, because some small slimes, like Mauve Slime, must be kept alive for many turns. Since these slimes start out with 1 Power and 2 Health, King Bee can’t attack it without killing it. For these slimes, the Creatures 2 Bees or not 2 Bees? and Beethoven can help, as these Creatures prevent the damage from 1-Power Creatures.
