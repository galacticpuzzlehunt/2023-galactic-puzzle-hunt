Solution: Spirit of the Vines

Written by Brian Chen and Violet Xiao

This puzzle is mostly about looking through our card collection and finding specific cards that can defeat these Creatures.

Sub-battle 1

We want one good card that can defeat a 2/4. Beethoven works.

Example solution: Bring a deck with a single Beethoven, then:

  • Turn 1: Play it anywhere.
  • Turn 2: Attack Miracle Growth with it.
  • Turn 3: Attack Miracle Growth with it.

Sub-battle 2

We want cards that can do more damage than their Power. Chocolate Calf works.

Example solution: Bring a deck with a single Chocolate Calf, then:

  • Turn 1: Play it anywhere.
  • Turn 2: Flex it, flex it, attack Leech Seed with it.
  • Turn 3: Flex it, flex it, attack Leech Seed with it.

Sub-battle 3

We want lots of Creatures that have 0 Power when they are summoned, and then some way to get them to do damage. Tusk Enhancement is the most likely way to solve it.

Example solution: Bring a deck with two Tusk Enhancements and four other Creatures with 0 Power that each cost 1 food (of any kind), then:

  • Turn 1: Play three non-Tusk Enhancement Creatures in column 2. End turn; Wild Weed will destroy the frontmost Creature.
  • Turn 2: Create with one of the surviving Creatures. Play the Tusk Enhancement in the frontmost slot. Attack with the other surviving Creature (dealing 1 Damage). End turn; Wild Weed will destroy the Tusk Enhancement.
  • Turn 3: Create with one of the surviving Creatures. Play the Tusk Enhancement in the frontmost slot. Attack with the other surviving Creature (dealing 2 Damage). End turn; Wild Weed will destroy the Tusk Enhancement.
  • Turn 4: Attack with either of the surviving Creatures.

Sub-battle 4

We can stabilize with Vanilla Calf’s Flex ability. Some amount of blitzing is also doable with e.g. Nightshade. Below we give a solution with only tutorial cards.

Example solution: Bring a deck with 2× Vanilla Calf, 2× Hog, and Yoshisaur Munchakoopas, then:

  • Turn 1: Play Vanilla Calf in R4C3 and Hog in R5C3.
  • Turn 2: Flex the Vanilla Calf twice, then Create with both it and Hog. Then play Vanilla Calf in R4C4 and Hog in R5C4.
  • Turn 3: Flex the damaged Vanilla Calf twice. Create with the R5C4 Hog and either (or both) Vanilla Calf. Attack with the R5C3 Hog. Play Yoshisaur Munchakoopas in R6C4.
  • Turn 4: Flex the damaged Vanilla Calf twice. Create with the R5C4 Hog and either Vanilla Calf. Attack with the R5C3 Hog, the other Vanilla Calf, and Yoshisaur Munchakoopas.
  • Turn 5: Flex the damaged Vanilla Calf twice. Create with the R5C4 Hog and either Vanilla Calf. Attack with the R5C3 Hog, the other Vanilla Calf, and Yoshisaur Munchakoopas.
  • Turn 6: Attack with Yoshisaur Munchakoopas, the R5C3 Hog, and either Vanilla Calf.

Sub-battle 5

We have to defeat Spirit of the Vines before defeating Overgrown Vines, without moving and becoming Sneaky. We can use Beeowulf to move Overgrown Vines out of the way, or cards like Roe Doe Dendron, Nightshade, Touch Grass, and Giraffatitan to ignore Protection.

Example solution: Bring a deck with 4 arbitrary Creatures that cost 1 Food (henceforth, “1-drops”), 2× Nightshade, 2× Ultra Violet. The following strategy depends on card draw, but it’s pretty likely: we need a Nightshade in our opening hand and an Ultra Violet in turn 3 that didn’t get randomly discarded in Turn 2 (which is guaranteed if the bottom card of your deck isn’t an Ultra Violet). Then:

  • Turn 1: Play 1-drop in front of Nightshade in front of Camp. End turn; Overgrown Vines attacks a Camp.
  • Turn 2: Play two cards (either 1-drops or Nightshade) on R4C1 and R4C5. Special with Nightshade targeting Spirit of the Vines. End turn; Spirit of the Vines destroys one of the R4 Creatures.
  • Turn 3: Create with both Creatures still on R4, leaving one Nightshade ready. Play Ultra Violet in whatever Space a Creature was destroyed in the previous turn (in particular, ensuring the Nightshade is protected). Special with Nightshade targeting Spirit of the Vines.
  • Turn 4: Special with Nightshade. Attack with Ultra Violet.
  • Turn 5+: Attack with Ultra Violet.
