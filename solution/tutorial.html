Solution: Tutorial

Written by Amon Ge, Colin Lu, Mark, CJ Quines, and Yannick Yao

Kero introduces us to GalactiCardCaptors, a card game where you battle foes to befriend them with the power of UWU.

Tutorial 1

Kero teaches the first basic Action: Attack.

Follow the tutorial to Attack with Yoshisaur Munchakoopas.

Befriended: Yoshisaur Munchakoopas, New Boarn

Tutorial 2

Kero teaches us how to win battles: Destroying all Enemy Bases.

Attack the Camp and End Turn. Hog and Hamlet Protect the Camp, but we can Attack the closest one on each turn before Destroying the Camp. Kero then teaches us about the five Factions of the cave: Boars, Dinos, Bees, Dryads, and Cows. They each like a different kind of Food, but everyone loves Pie.

Befriended: Hamlet, Hog

Tutorial 3

Kero teaches us the remaining basic Actions: Move and Create, as well as how to Summon cards from our hand.

The battle expands to a 6x5 board!

  • Move Yoshisaur in front of our Camp to Protect it, end turn
  • Attack one Beeowulf, end turn
  • Attack the other Beeowulf, end turn

The Cows are coming, so we let’s Summon our new Boar friends:

  • Summon Hog, end turn
  • Summon Hamlet, end turn
  • Create two Boarries with Hog and Hamlet.
  • Summon New Boarn, end turn

On Turn 7, Three Chocolate Calfs appear. It’s useful to make a save here in case we mess up. There are many variations to win, but the basic strategy is the same: Destroy the Chocolate Calfs first and attack the Camps.

Here’s one way to win if we Summoned a Hamlet in front of our Camp:

  • Let Hamlet Create a Boarry
  • Let New Boarn Attack a Chocolate Calf in front of an Enemy Camp, consuming the Boarry
  • Let Yoshisaur Attack the exposed Camp, consuming the Pie gained from our Camp
  • End turn. Yoshisaur and Hog are Destroyed.
  • (Turn 8:) Let New Boarn Attack a Chocolate Calf
  • End turn. Hamlet is Destroyed.
  • (Turn 9:) Let New Boarn Attack the last Chocolate Calf.
  • (Turn 10+:) There are no more enemies, so we can freely Attack the remaining Camps with New Boarn.

Befriended: Chocolate Calf, Beeowulf

Tutorial 4

Kero teaches us about Special and Flex.

  • Summon Chocolate Calf and Beeowulf anywhere. End turn.
  • Use Beeowulf’s Special. Select 2 Bees or not 2 Bees? and select a Space left or right of it. This will Move 2 Bees or not 2 Bees?, exposing the enemy Camp
  • Use Chocolate Calf’s Flex to gain 1 Power. Let Chocolate Calf Attack the Camp.

Befriended: PBee & J, 2 Bees or not 2 Bees?

Tutorial 5

Kero teaches us about Sneaky.

This battle teaches basic battle strategy:

  • Build up a board of Creatures by Summoning cheap Creatures like Hamlet, Hog, Beeowulf, PBee & Js in front of stronger Creatures like Yoshisaur, Chocolate Calf, and New Boarn, and in front of our Camps.

  • Don’t let our Camps get Destroyed, since we lose a lot of momentum without the Pie they provide.

  • Attack the threatening Enemy Creatures: Roe Doe Dendron and Nepeta Legion. It’s useful to Create Food before Attacking with the same Faction Creatures to save Pies for Summoning.

  • Once we have a stronger board than the opponent, we can start Destroying Camps in addition to Roe Doe Dendron and Nepeta Legion. Optionally, we can Move our Creatures into Enemy Spaces to be Sneaky and attack even Protected Camps.

Befriended: Flora the Explora, Roe Doe Dendron, Nepeta Legion

Tutorial 6

Kero teaches us about deckbuilding, and Kero’s Notes.

  • Make a deck with our new friends!
  • Summon a bunch of them.
  • Attack the exposed Camp to win!

Befriended: o, Payne, re, Vanilla Calf, Chicken, Petroleum Jelly, Log, Beethoven

The tutorial is meant to be challenging ramp-up to the game. Before we can start breaking the rules, we have to teach them! We hope you trusted us throwing you “in the deep end”, since the first battles to unlock (Angry Boarry Farmer and Dargle) immediately test your understanding of the game.

This is also the puzzle that went through the most revisions through hunt-writing. The earliest tutorial required you to manually change the URL (from tutorial1 to tutorial2, and so on), and had a secret level past the final level.
