bitsy, conceptually, is a polar opposite to PuzzleScript. PuzzleScript is mechanics-driven to its core, capable of demaking complex turn based puzzle games like Patrick‚Äôs Parabox and Baba Is You. bitsy has almost no interactive mechanics besides movement and dialogue, while games made in PuzzleScript are rarely narrative-driven.

The two engines really are more similar than they are different. Both engines are designed to be an accessible tool for making small games.
