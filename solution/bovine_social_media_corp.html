Solution: Bovine Social Media Corp
Answer: EJECTION

Written by Violet Xiao

The posts of this puzzle depict employees of a social media company talking about the rebrand of their company. In fact, this puzzle is a reference of the recent rebrand of Twitter to X. Each post has two components, something the employee used to be, and something that they will be.

The former describes a word, and the latter describes the same word with X put in the front. The word fits the blanks, with the marked blank used for extraction. These words, in post order, are

Word Extracted Letter
GAMES E
JAPAN J
FILES E
FACTOR C
RATED T
WING I
BOX O
MEN N

The answer is EJECTION, a change that some of the employees of the recently rebranded company experienced.
